Fox

Foxes use Earth's magnetic field to hunt their prey

  • Most common specie: Red Fox
  • Most similar to: Cats
  • Lifestyle: Solitary
  • Trait: Playful

Foxes live in many diverse habitats around the world - including forests, grasslands, mountains, and deserts. They adapt well to human environments such as farms, suburban areas, and even large communities. The red fox's resourcefulness has earned it a legendary reputation for intelligence and cunning.
